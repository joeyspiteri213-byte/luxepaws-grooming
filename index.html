Cancellation, Rescheduling & No-Show Policy

At Luxe Paws Grooming Salon, appointment times are reserved specifically for you and your pet. Because grooming services require dedicated staff and preparation, we ask that all clients follow the policy below.

Rescheduling Policy:

Clients may reschedule appointments with at least 24 hours’ notice without penalty.

Rescheduling with less than the required notice may be treated as a late cancellation.

Late Cancellation Fee:

Cancellations made within less than 24 hours will be subject to a fee of 50% of the scheduled service cost.

No-Show Policy:

No-shows will be charged 100% of the grooming service fee.

After two no-shows, clients may be required to prepay or be declined future bookings.

Late Arrival Policy:

If you are more than 15 minutes late, we may shorten or reschedule your appointment. Full fee still applies.

Deposits:

Deposits are required and are non-refundable within the 24-hour period.

Emergency Exceptions:

Fees may be waived in cases of medical emergencies, severe weather, or unexpected situations.

Thank you for your understanding. These policies help us provide the best care for every pet.
