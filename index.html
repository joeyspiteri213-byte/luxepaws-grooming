Welcome to Luxe Paws Grooming, your local dog grooming salon in Fraser Rise. We're passionate about providing gentle, professional, and stress-free grooming services in a safe and caring environment.

Every dog that walks through our doors is treated like family — with comfort, patience, and individual attention at the heart of everything we do. Whether your fur baby is here for a full groom, a tidy-up, or a fresh wash, our goal is to have them looking and feeling their very best.

We proudly welcome dogs of all breeds, sizes, and personalities, committed to creating a positive grooming experience every single time.

All Breeds Welcome

From tiny terriers to large breeds — every dog receives the same attentive care.

Stress-Free Environment

Our salon is designed for calm, comfort, and a relaxed grooming experience.

Individual Attention

No rushing. Every appointment is given the time your pet truly deserves.

Salon Policies

Terms & Conditions

✦

Appointment times are reserved specifically for you and your pet. Because grooming services require dedicated staff and preparation, we ask that all clients follow the policy below.

Rescheduling

We understand that plans change. Clients may reschedule with at least 24 hours' notice without penalty. Rescheduling with less notice may be treated as a late cancellation.

Late Cancellation Fee

Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment will incur a fee of 50% of the scheduled service cost — this helps cover the time reserved for your pet.

No-Show Policy

  • No-shows are charged 100% of the grooming service fee
  • After two no-shows, prepayment may be required
  • Repeat no-shows may result in declined future bookings

Late Arrival

Please arrive on time. If you are more than 15 minutes late, we may need to shorten the session or reschedule. Full-service fees still apply.

Deposits

A deposit is required to secure your booking and will be applied to your final total. Cancellations within the 24-hour notice period are non-refundable.

Emergency Exceptions

Fees may be waived at management discretion for medical emergencies, severe weather, or unexpected family situations. Please contact us as soon as possible.
