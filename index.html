About Luxe Paws Grooming

Welcome to Luxe Paws Grooming, your local dog grooming salon in Fraser Rise! We’re passionate about providing gentle, professional, and stress-free grooming services in a safe and caring environment. Every dog that walks through our doors is treated like family, with comfort, patience, and individual attention at the heart of everything we do. Whether your fur baby is here for a full groom, a tidy-up, or just a fresh wash, our goal is to have them looking and feeling their very best. We proudly welcome dogs of all breeds, sizes, and personalities, and we’re committed to creating a positive grooming experience every time. Thank you for choosing Luxe Paws Grooming — we can’t wait to meet you and your furry companion!
